Laminar MHD flow in a square duct with electrically conductive Hartmann walls (walls perpendicular to the magnetic field). Wall conductivity ratio Cd = 0.1. Re = 500, Ha = 5000.

Cd = (sigma_solid * solid_characteristc_scale) / (sigma fluid * fluid_characteristc_scale)
where:

sigma = electrical conductivity
solid_characteristc_scale = thickness of the solid wall
fluid_characteristc_scale = width of the fluid domain

Please adjust numerical settings and time scale.
